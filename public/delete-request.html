User Data Deletion Instructions

If you have linked your Facebook or Instagram account to our platform and wish to delete your associated data, you can do so at any time.

To request deletion of your data:

Login to your account on our platform.
Navigate to your Account Settings or Dashboard.
Click the “Delete Facebook/Instagram Data” button.

This will immediately delete all Facebook- and Instagram-related data associated with your account from our servers.
